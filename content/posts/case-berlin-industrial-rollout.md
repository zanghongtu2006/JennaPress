A company website often needs a place for proof-heavy storytelling. That is why the **Cases** category exists in this demo.

Instead of building a separate CMS type for customer stories, the same post model can stay in place. The category becomes the switch that changes the list card rhythm, the section framing, and the article mood.

## Why this matters

- Agencies can ship one backend model.
- Editors only learn one post schema.
- Frontend teams still get room to design distinct modules.
