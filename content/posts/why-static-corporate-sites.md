For most official company websites, the public experience is simple: visitors read pages, explore product information, and maybe click a contact channel. There is usually no need for account systems, order flows, or a custom admin backend.

That means the website can often be delivered as static output and deployed through a CDN-first platform such as Cloudflare Pages.

## What becomes easier

- There is no database to maintain.
- There is no admin login surface to secure.
- SEO stays predictable because every public page is prerendered.
- Template reuse becomes easier for agencies and freelancers.

The real work shifts toward schema design, template discipline, and a stable content contract.
